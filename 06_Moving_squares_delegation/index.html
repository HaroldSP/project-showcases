On each cell, you see buttons with directions.

When you click on a button, the cell changes its position with the corresponding cell in the direction of the arrow.

If there is nowhere to go when clicking up, down, right, or left, nothing should happen.

When clicking the RESET button, all cells should return to their original positions.
